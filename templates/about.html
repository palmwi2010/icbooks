Why ICBooks?

ICBooks was set up by five Imperial Masters of Computing students to solve a problem we had all experienced. Today, the lifetime of a book from purchase to an eternity on a bookshelf is short. You buy a book, read it once, and most likely never touch it again.


This is bad...

  • For the reader: After buying a new book, you get no residual value once reading it, and this makes reading unnecessarily expensive.
  • For the book: It is a sad existence for the book to live out eternity having only been read once.
  • For the environment: If books are read by one person only, we need to print more, and that means cutting down more trees.

ICBooks is here to change this

By using ICBooks, you can swap a book you have read for someone else's. Everyone wins, and you don't need to worry about negotiating on money - this is a straight swap! We decided to focus on students first, who today struggle the most to balance their passion for reading with the constraints of a student budget.


We're starting right here at Imperial College, so if you see a book you like, just Email the owner and organise a swapping point on campus! And if you don't study here today, worry not - we will be expanding our reach to many universities across the UK!
